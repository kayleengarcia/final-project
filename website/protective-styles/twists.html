Twists

This is image #9 in Ive's photo diary. A Twist is a hairstyle, also known as "two-strand twist" or a "double-strand twist." The technique is similar to braiding except twisting the hair is used. A popular hairstyle for both women and men the twist is great for thick, frizzy and coarse hair, but works on any hair.
