Intense Hair Therapy Using the Baggy Method

1). Using your oil mixture in an applicator bottle, directly apply the oil to your scalp.

 

2). Then wet your fingertips in oil too and scalp massage your head with the balls of your fingertips, as gently / roughly as you feel like. Do this for as long as you please. Ten to twenty minutes is fine, but really base it on how you are feeling.

 

3). Flip your head over for four minutes while simultaneously massaging the scalp. This allows for fresh blood to rush to your head, which stimulates the hair follicles and promotes accelerated hair growth.

 

4). Style your hair in any protective hairstyle (i.e. braids, bun, etc.)

 

5). Put a plastic cap on, and your silk wrap and / or beanie to sleep with. This will create a hot oil treatment with your own body heat. (Please note: You do not have to sleep with the treatment; you can do this when ever you please. Just be sure to include this vital step.)

 

6). When you remove the cap, your hair will be wet. This is how you know the baggy
method has worked. The oil penetrates each strand of hair, keeping the hair moisturized.

 

7). Depending on what you want to do: you can either wash your hair out, or just
condition.

 

If you decide to shampoo the oil out, be sure to only target the scalp. Regardless if you are trying or not, the suds will clean your hair on their own, without you doing anything to your actual tresses. Shampoo dries up our hair, so we want to be particular about our wash day.

 

The oil will promote lots of slip for not only when you condition your hair in the shower, but also for when you are doing the scalp massage. This will allow you to finger detangle your hair, and minimize the amount of hair that is breaking. Be sure to keep your hair untangled all of the time, if possible.
